# Listing package deliverables

Use this contract for the final package. Omit a section only when it is outside the requested stores or locales, and state why.

## 1. Evidence inventory

| Claim or input | Evidence | Status | Usage restriction |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product capability | Build, source, or approved product brief | Verified / assumed / missing | Fields and locales where usable |

List rejected or softened claims with the reason.

## 2. Platform matrix

| Store | Locale | Field or asset | Current constraint | Official source | Checked date |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Google Play or Apple App Store | Locale code | Field name | Exact verified requirement | Direct official link | ISO date |

Keep volatile requirements here, not permanently embedded in the skill.

## 3. Positioning

- Primary audience and job to be done
- Core promise
- Supporting value pillars and proof
- Message order
- Voice and prohibited language
- Keyword themes and intent

## 4. Icon concepts

| Concept | Metaphor and silhouette | Composition and palette | Small-size behavior | Risks | Production prompt |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

End with a recommended concept and evidence-based rationale. A concept is not a guarantee of approval or performance.

## 5. Screenshot storyboard

| Frame | Objective | Headline | Support | Product screen/state | Composition | Localization and legibility | Production prompt |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Add an asset manifest mapping every final file to store, locale, device class, orientation, dimensions, format, and source file.

## 6. Copy matrix

| Store | Locale | Field | Final copy | Count/limit | Keyword purpose | Evidence note |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Include alternate copy only when it represents a real experiment or locale need, not filler.

## 7. Localization notes

For every locale record terminology, tone, search-intent differences, text expansion, typography, cultural/legal risks, screenshot substitutions, and reviewer questions.

## 8. Experiment backlog

| Priority | Single-variable hypothesis | Variant | Primary metric | Guardrail | Minimum decision rule |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|

Do not estimate uplift without relevant historical evidence.

## 9. Submission preflight

- Product facts and claims are evidenced.
- Current official constraints were checked and linked.
- Icon and screenshots match the live product and platform policy.
- Text fits verified limits without truncation.
- Required disclosures and legal copy are present.
- Locale-specific assets and metadata are mapped.
- Source files, export names, formats, and color profiles are recorded.
- Accessibility and small-size legibility have been reviewed.
- Ownership and upload status are explicit.
